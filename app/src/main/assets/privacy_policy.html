Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

At Littichokha Studio, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how AI Background Remover handles your data — or rather, how it doesn't collect any.

No Data Collection

AI Background Remover is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

✨ All image processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos never leave your phone.

Offline Processing

Our app uses on-device machine learning to remove backgrounds from your photos. This means:

  • No internet connection is required for core functionality
  • Your images are never uploaded to any server
  • Processing happens instantly on your device
  • Your photos remain 100% private

Permissions We Use

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos directly within the app (optional)
  • Storage/Photos: To access and save images to your gallery

These permissions are used solely to provide app functionality and are never used to collect or share your data.

Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies or similar technologies. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information about how ad data is handled.

We do not share any personal information with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.
